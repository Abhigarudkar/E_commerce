Crop Guide

Tailored organic solutions for optimal yield.

Sugarcane

Requires consistent nitrogen. Premium Vermicompost boosts cane thickness and yield.

  • Season: All Year
  • Water: High
  • Fertilizer: ~150 kg/Acre
  • Growth: 12-18 Months

Cotton

Prone to pests. Pure Neem Cake provides essential nutrients while keeping soil pests away.

  • Season: Kharif
  • Water: Medium
  • Fertilizer: ~50 kg/Acre
  • Growth: 5-6 Months

Onion & Garlic

Bone Meal Powder provides the essential phosphorus needed for strong bulb development.

  • Season: Rabi & Kharif
  • Water: Medium
  • Fertilizer: ~50 kg/Acre
  • Growth: 3-4 Months

Jowar & Bajra

Hardy crops that thrive well with a solid base of Aged Cow Dung Compost.

  • Season: Kharif & Rabi
  • Water: Low
  • Fertilizer: ~30 kg/Acre
  • Growth: 3.5-4 Months
